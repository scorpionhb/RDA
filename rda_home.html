Welcome users!
Aberdeen RDA is a member group of the national charity Riding for the Disabled Association (RDA). The Aberdeen Group was formed in 1970 and is the oldest group in Scotland.
Aberdeen RDA is based at Aberdeen Riding Centre, Milltimber. Thanks to the help of over 50 volunteers, Aberdeen RDA provides riding opportunities for around 50 riders each week.
