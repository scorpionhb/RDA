Information about future volunteers:
Aberdeen RDA is run entirely by volunteers. No previous experience of working with the disabled or with horses is necessary as training is given to all volunteers.
The main roles of a volunteer including leading the pony (leader) and walking alongside the rider (side-helper). We just need people who are reasonably physically fit and happy to help - even when it is cold!
We only require people to help out once a week at one session, which is a maximum of 2.5 hours.
